Our Mission & Vision

Bestworld Express has set up its mission to become the biggest E-Commerce site in Bangladesh within 2019 and to become the famous one in the globe within the year 2020. Bangladesh is a land of natural herbs. We are producing some uncompromising quality standards Agro-Natural foods in Bangladesh by using our own herbs. Within a very short time we will set up world class export oriented Agro- Natural industry here. For E-commerce development we have added the Electric & Electronics, Fashion products, Kitchen appliances, Leather goods, Health items and consumer goods in our product list.

We have focused on making human resources skilled with best-in-class technology, innovative thinking. Driven by the ramifications of this success Bestworld Express geared up to invest in new fields, including manufacturing and trading. The Group now has over 4 major concerns in different sectors. BestCure, BWEX, BWTC and the mother concern "Bestworld Express Ltd". The multi-faceted shopping mall called the "Bestworld City" will be added glamour to the growth of the group. The "Bestworld Express" is one step ahead in the longstanding effort to strengthen links with the general people through the unique offering of commercial operations and recreation facilities under single roof.

‘The Bestworld Express has come a long way in reaching its goals by listening to client needs, learning real-time lessons from past projects, innovating and partnering its project implementation process. Through major investment undertakings in all key sectors, Bestworld Express Ltd. has meaningfully contributed to the country’s economic stability. Now the name "Bestworld” inspires trust.
